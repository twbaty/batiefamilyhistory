<h2>About Batie Family History</h2>

<p>This blog documents genealogical research into the Batie/Baty family, originating with Thomas Batie of Northumberland, England, around 1720.</p>

<p>Research includes vital records, military service, census data, cemetery records, and family stories spanning several generations and branches of the family tree.</p>
