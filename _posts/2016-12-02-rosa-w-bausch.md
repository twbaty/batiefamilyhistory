<br />
Rosa W Bausch - mother-in-law of &nbsp;Ina V Armfield, my 2nd Cousin 3x Removed.<br />
<br />
Rosa was born in Germany in 1854. &nbsp;She came to America in 1871. &nbsp;In 1875 she married James Henry Brayton in Chicago, IL.<br />
<br />
I have conflicting information on her death. &nbsp;On one hand, I have found her will that was penned 9 Jul 1916. &nbsp;On the other hand, I have a probate record that says she passed 19 Oct 1915; this date was recorded in 1923.<br />
<br />
This is certainly Rosa. &nbsp;In the will, she references her son George A Brayton. &nbsp;As well, the probate record references him as the executor.<br />
<br />
I get that we should trust records that were recorded while the person was alive over records after their death. &nbsp;The problem is that the dates aren't even close! &nbsp;In the absence of a death certificate, I'm stumped on exactly what date is accurate.<br />
<br />
I'm trying to get to the bottom of just what her date of death was. &nbsp;Any help or thoughts are appreciated.<br />
