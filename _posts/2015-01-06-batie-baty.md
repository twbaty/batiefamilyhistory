In "<i>The Batie Family History</i>", Howard chronicles that Thomas Batie (bap. 1783) changed his last name to "Baty" when he emigrated to Canada in 1819. &nbsp;As did his dad, Arthur. &nbsp;Where it gets confusing is that his brother Adam continues with the spelling "Batie". &nbsp;As I wonder with everything, "Why?". &nbsp; What was it about the emigration that caused Thomas to change his last name?

Let's look at some events taken from "<i>The Batie Family History</i>":

26 Apr 1782 - Arthur (C) marries Sisley Telfer. &nbsp;He signs his name on the on the marriage register as "Batie", As did his brother John (D).

1819 - Thomas (CA) and his wife Margaret emigrate to Ontario, Canada in 1819. &nbsp;Thomas and his father Arthur (C) change the spelling of their last name from Batie to Baty.

1824 - John (D) emigrates to Ontario Canada. &nbsp;Maintains the spelling of "Batie"
