<br />
<br />
I was on Ancestry exploring the "hints" on some folks, &nbsp;Started in on Adam Telfer (<i>BCF</i>) (1831-1879) - I didn't have his date of death and was excited to find the death record. &nbsp;I'm really trying to get to no available "hints" - but I fear that will be a LONG time in the coming.<br />
<br />
It's hard to say what I find more interesting; knowing how someone lived, or when someone lived. &nbsp;I find I go back and forth. &nbsp;For Adam, the when of it caught me. <br />
<br />
He died 21 Oct 1879 at 48 years old. &nbsp;He was a merchant. &nbsp;Looks like T.S. Telfer was the informant - wonder who that is.... &nbsp;Got to the cause of death - "suicide cut throat with razor".<br />
<br />
OK, now I wonder how he lived. &nbsp;What could have caused him to have taken his own life? &nbsp;And in such a deliberate way. &nbsp;What was going on that was that bad?<br />
