I was working through trying to synchronize two trees.&nbsp;&nbsp; I found Dwayne A Batie was one of those that wasn't in both trees.

Sadly, it seems I'm finding more and more family members meeting their demise at a young age.&nbsp; Dwayne is no different.

While I don't have a lot of information on Dwayne, the one thing I was able to find was a newspaper article explaining the events of his death.

What I have found is that Dwayne A Batie was born in 1916.&nbsp; In 1933, his young life was brought to an untimely end.

RIP Dwayne....
