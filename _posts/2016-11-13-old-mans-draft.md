<h3 style="background: none rgb(255, 255, 255); border-bottom: none; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 1.2em; line-height: 1.6; margin: 0.3em 0px 0px; overflow: hidden; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-top: 0.5em;">
<span class="mw-headline" id="Fourth_Registration_.28Old_Man.27s_Draft.29">Fourth Registration (Old Man's Draft)</span></h3>

<div style="background-color: white; color: #252525; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: inherit; margin-bottom: 0.5em; margin-top: 0.5em;">
<b>Included men with a date of birth from April 28, 1877 to February 16, 1897.</b></div>

<div style="background-color: white; color: #252525; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: inherit; margin-bottom: 0.5em; margin-top: 0.5em;">
On April 27, 1942, the Selective Service conducted the fourth of six draft registrations. The "World War II Selective Service Draft Cards: Fourth Registration, 1942" is often referred to as the “Old Man’s Registration” or the “Old Man’s Draft".</div>

<div style="background-color: white; color: #252525; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: inherit; margin-bottom: 0.5em; margin-top: 0.5em;">
This registration was different from the others conducted by the government.&nbsp; It's sole purpose was to gather information regarding manpower resources in the United States.&nbsp; The registration included men born between 27 April 1877 and 16 February 1890 - ages 45 to 64 - and listed their industrial capacity and skills that could be used for military support during World War II.</div>

<div style="background-color: white; color: #252525; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: inherit; margin-bottom: 0.5em; margin-top: 0.5em;">
See a description of the Fourth Registration Draft Cards from the National Archives at&nbsp;draft-cards-fourth-registration.pdf.</div>

<h4 style="background: none rgb(255, 255, 255); border-bottom: none; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.6; margin: 0.3em 0px 0px; overflow: hidden; padding-bottom: 0px; padding-top: 0.5em;">
