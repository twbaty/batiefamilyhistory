Back in the '70s,&nbsp;it might have been the 80's, my uncle&nbsp;opened a restaurant in Fort Smith. &nbsp;Perhaps it was more of a bar. &nbsp;I have vague memories of it. &nbsp;My dad and him weren't close, but I loved the name.<br />
<br />
Tom Foolery's<br />
<br />
I remember a small white bathtub with the name "Tom Foolery's" on it. &nbsp;I liked that little bathtub. &nbsp;I don't know much about the restaurant itself. &nbsp;And by the time I got old enough to begin asking about it, it no longer existed. &nbsp;I'm not sure if my uncle sold it for other ventures....
