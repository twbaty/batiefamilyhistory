Clifford Wiggins Pickering was born in 1898 in Labette County Kansas to Luther Hess and Mary Elizabeth (BATY) Pickering.

While turning a drove of mules, he was kicked in the side.&nbsp; He developed peritonitis causing his untimely death at the age of 15 years old.
