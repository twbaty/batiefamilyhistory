I started researching Steven Wayne Bradbury about 2 years ago. &nbsp;It was rough going &nbsp;I found some information that provided some information.It's intriguing, but I cannot find official records of Steven Bradbury.

Some information provided by Fold3.com <b>(1)</b> tells us that Steven died in Vietnam on 13 Jul 1969.

Death: 13 Jul 1969

Cause: Other Explosive Device

Age at Death: 20

Body Recovered: Recovered

Casualty Date: 13 Jul 1969

Casualty Type: Hostile, Died

But it doesn't tell us the story of Steven's death.

I also found "The Virtual Wall" and a note:
