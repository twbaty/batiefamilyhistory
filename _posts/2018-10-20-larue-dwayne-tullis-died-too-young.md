I was working hints in Ancestry and came across Larue Dwayne Tullis.&nbsp; Larue was only 11 years old when he passed away.&nbsp; I looked and looked for some kind of newspaper article explaining what happened.&nbsp; I wasn't able to find an online resource with information about his death.&nbsp; Was he playing in the street?&nbsp; Did the car veer?&nbsp; Was it a parking lot accident?

Here is the obituary I've found associated with Larue's death.

<div class="mediaStoryBody ng-binding" data-ng-bind-html="trustAsHtml(item.story)" data-ng-show="item.story || item.ext === 'x-inline'">

<blockquote>
<span style="font-size: small;">BARTLETT
 - The funeral last Thursday afternoon of Larue Tullis was perhaps the 
largest gathering of relatives ever to attend a funeral here. All except
 two seats in the main body of the church were filled with relatives of 
the boy. His schoolmates from Foland rural school served as pall bearers
 and flower bearers. Larue, 11-year old son of Mr. and Mrs. R.B. Tullis 
died last Tuesday of injuries suffered when he was struck by an 
automobile while playing with his schoolmates during the noon hour.
- Chetopa Advance (Chetopa, KS), January 5, 1939</span></blockquote>

It's always unfortunate when someone is taken too soon.

RIP Larue!
