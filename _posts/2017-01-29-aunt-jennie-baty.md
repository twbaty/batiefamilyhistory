Saw you post about who we call Aunt Jennie Baty.  I grew up in Bartlett and there is an old story that everyone knows.  Aunt Jennie approached Dr. Randall at his drug store in Bartlett and asked for a donation to the Presbyterian Church.  Dr. Randall was a crusty old soul and replied, "No, I don't think so.  I am planning on taking all my money with me when I die".  Aunt Jennie, always ready, replied, "well where you are going it will all burn up"!<br abp="24" /><br abp="25" />Norman R.
