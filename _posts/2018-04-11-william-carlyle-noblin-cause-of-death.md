The purpose of this blog is to tell about interesting finds within the Batie Family History.&nbsp; This time I found an item in a companion tree, Franklin Family History.&nbsp;<br />
<br />
William Carlyle Noblin, my 2nd cousin twice removed on my mom's side.&nbsp; His death certificate indicates he worked in the ice cream industry as a 'route man'.&nbsp; I guess that means he drove an ice cream truck.<br />
<br />
He passed away in 1960, on either June 23rd or 24th.&nbsp; The confusion on the date of death is interesting.&nbsp; Reading further, he was last attended on 2 July 1960 with a note that he was DOA.<br />
<br />
Again.&nbsp; Why?&nbsp; Why is there such a gap between when he was said to have died and when he was last attended?<br />
<br />
The cause of death is listed as<br />
<br />
<i>"Brain damage</i><br />
<i>Fractured Skull"</i><br />
<br />
The injury occurred by<br />
<br />
<i>"Deceased was struck back of head</i><br />
<i>with blunt instrument during robbery"</i><br />
<br />
That's rough, getting robbed in an ice cream truck.&nbsp; And killed for your efforts.<br />
