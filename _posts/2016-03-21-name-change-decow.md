Donald Frank DeCow.

We have traced Donald's roots back to 1826 in Ontario, Canada.&nbsp; Somewhere before 1920 he changed his name.

His dad's headstone lists 'Frank S DeCow'.&nbsp; 

One brother's headstone lists 'Clyde DeCow'

Another brother's headstone lists 'Harold J DeCow'

I just heard information that Donald changed his name due to his job - a professor. &nbsp;I don't have all the details yet, but I'm working on them.
