Dennis Gwenn Workman.&nbsp; My 4th cousin 1x removed, on my dad's side.&nbsp;<br />
<br />
Dennis has several records out there demonstrating different dates of death.<br />
<br />
If you look at his headstone, he was born, and passed away, on 20 Sep 1959.<br />

So, which one is correct?&nbsp; If you go by the genealogical standard, the record that occurred closet to death is likely the most accurate.&nbsp; In this case, it's clear the death certificate occurred prior to the headstone.</div>
