I was working on family history this weekend and discovered Ronald Lee Fields, my 3rd Cousin 2x removed. &nbsp;Ronald had three children, according to his obituary. &nbsp;One child, Kami, had passed away before Ronald did. &nbsp;His obituary did not mention a wife. &nbsp;This put me on the hunt. <br />
<br />
Who did he marry?<br />
Why wasn't she mentioned in the obituary?<br />
What happened to Kami?<br />
What happened to his wife?<br />
<br />
I searched Ancestry.com, but did not find anything within a few searches. &nbsp;I then headed over to Newspapers.com. &nbsp;Fortunately, Kami is a fairly unique name. &nbsp;Bingo! &nbsp;I found the unfortunate news...<br />
