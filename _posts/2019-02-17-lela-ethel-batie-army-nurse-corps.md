Lela Ethel Batie (<span style="font-family: &quot;Arial&quot;,sans-serif; font-size: 11.0pt; layout-grid-mode: line; letter-spacing: -.1pt; mso-ansi-language: EN-US; mso-bidi-language: AR-SA; mso-fareast-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; mso-fareast-language: EN-US;">CGCDA</span>) 1886-1976, is my 3rd Cousin, 3x Removed.<br />
<br />
Lela is the daughter of Charles Henry Batie.<br />
<br />
In 1917, at the start of WWI, physicians from Nebraska recognized the need for medical care for the military serving in France.&nbsp; They were able to collect $100,000 to fund their mission.<br />
<br />
Base Hospital 49 was up and running in August of 1918 in Allerey, France.<br />
<br />
Lela left for Base Hospital 49 on 26 Aug 1918.<br />
<br />

<br />
The Hospital ended operations in Jan 1919, a few months after the Armistice was signed ending WWI on 11 Nov, 1918.<br />
<br />
When Lela returned home, she worked as a school nurse.<br />
