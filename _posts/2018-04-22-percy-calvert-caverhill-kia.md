Percy Calvert Caverhill, BDDAD, joined the Canadian Over-seas Expeditionary Force 4 Nov 1915.&nbsp; His enlistment papers list him as a "Student (Theology).

On 3 Jul 1916, Percy penned a military will.

Percy died in action 9 Nov 1916 near Martinpuich, France.&nbsp; He was a member of the 11th Brigade, Canadian Field Artillery.

According to the official KIA record,

Percy is buried in the Adanac Military Cemetery in Miraumont France.&nbsp; His <a href="https://www.findagrave.com/memorial/56553946" target="_blank">memorial </a>can be found on <a href="https://www.findagrave.com/memorial/56553946">Findagrave.com</a>.

As of this writing, the Canadian Virtual War Memorial has a <a href="http://www.veterans.gc.ca/eng/remembrance/memorials/canadian-virtual-war-memorial/detail/183006?Percy%20Calvert%20Caverhill">memorial for Percy</a>.
